About Us

Going Green with EV

AZoom Car Rental

Here at AZoom Car Rental, we specializes in renting electric cars for the public. We have an attractive range of electric cars available to suit different needs and occasions for our environmentally conscious customers.

By choosing to drive an EV you are helping to reduce harmful air pollution from exhaust emissions. An electric car has zero greenhouse emissions because it relies on a battery to power its motor and does not burn any fuel internally. It also requires less of the more harmful, contaminating liquids like engine oil and coolant fluids.
